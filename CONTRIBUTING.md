Thank you for helping us keep this guide current and accurate! The digital world moves fast, and your input is invaluable for parents.

## How to Contribute

1.  **Fork** this repository.
2.  **Create a new branch** for your update (e.g., `feat/add-new-terms`, `fix/clarify-sus`).
3.  **Make your changes** in the `README.md` file.
4.  **Commit** your changes with a clear message (e.g., `Added five new terms to the 'Humor' section`).
5.  **Submit a Pull Request (PR)** back to the main branch.

## Contribution Standards

To maintain the quality and focus of the guide, please follow these guidelines:

1.  **Format:** All submissions must be in the format: `**term** --- definition (Category)`.
2.  **Category:** Ensure the new term is placed under the most appropriate category (e.g., Affection, Gaming, Humor, Warnings).
3.  **Focus:** The guide is focused on *digital* communication and slang that is primarily used by tweens/teens.
4.  **Verification:** Please ensure the term is *currently* in use by verifying its usage across social media or messaging platforms.
5.  **Clarity:** Definitions must be concise, accurate, and easy for a non-digital native parent to understand.
